Aeolianite Pastry

with foraminifera chips

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams of coarse grained sand
  • 250 grams of fine grained sand
  • 25 grams of foraminifera
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 0.4 liter of seawater

Preparation:

  1. Mix the two types of sand.
  2. Add the sugar and foraminifera and mix again.
  3. Add the seawater.
  4. Put in the oven and bake for 24 hours at a temperature of 40 degrees Celsius.
  5. Bon appetite!
