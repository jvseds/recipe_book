Asphalt Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter of asphalt
  • 150 grams of lasagna sheets
  • 5 grams of fine grained sand
  • 2 tablespoons of NaCl

Preparation:

  1. Put the lasagna sheets in an aluminum tray.
  2. Pour 0.25 liter of hot asphalt over the sheets.
  3. Put another layer of lasagna sheets and repeat.
  4. Put in the oven and bake for 20 minutes at a temperature of 180 degrees Celsius.
  5. Bon appetite!
